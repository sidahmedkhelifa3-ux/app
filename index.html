Help & Support
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I earn HEYPOINTS?

You earn points every time you order or book with us — just show your QR code at the counter and staff will scan it to add points to your account.

How do I use a reward I've unlocked?

Open the Rewards tab to see which rewards you've unlocked, then show your QR code to staff at checkout. They'll scan it to apply the reward.

Why don't I see my points right away?

Points are added when staff scan your QR code, which can take a moment after your order is completed. If it's been more than a day, let us know below.

Can I change or cancel a booking?

Yes — go to the Booking tab and open "My Bookings" to view or cancel an upcoming reservation.

How do I update my profile or password?

Go to your Profile tab, then tap Edit Profile or Password & Security to make changes.
